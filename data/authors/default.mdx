Haider Ali Punjabi is a software developer from Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir. He is known for his work in web and Android app development as well as his contributions to open source projects.

His works include [CovidKashmir](https://covidkashmir.org) - A website tracking COVID-19 cases in Jammu & Kashmir, along with providing related information & visualizations, [Iftarkar](https://iftarkar.com) - A web app for Ramazan Timings, to which he later also added [Namazpar](https://namazpar.com) - A cross platform mobile application for Prayer and Ramazan Timings. 

He is also the developer of [Tweet2Pic](https://tweet2pic.app) - An Android App that converts Twitter / X Tweets to images with many editing capabilities.
